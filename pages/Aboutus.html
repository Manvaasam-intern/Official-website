Meet our CEO

On 14th February 2020, MANVAASAM was started by a group of Farmers, Students and working professionals as an organization and are involved in Social, Environmental, Training and Technical Activities. Agricultural activities include Agri app, Selling Agricultural Based Products(Flipkart,instagram,webpage- manvaasam.in), Hiring Farmers, Public Events, Manvaasam courier services. Technical activities like webpage and mobile development, Creating innovative machines(Plastic recycling etc), Provide security to organization, Logo/Poster/Videos Creation and Training activities include Demo interviews, Online Training Courses (Free /paid), Webinars (Free /paid), Paid workshops, Community Services (Free /paid), Internships.
