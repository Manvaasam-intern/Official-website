Agri Application

Agri enables Users or Farmers to buy or sell the products based on the daily price rate of the product which is continuously updated by our team. Through this app, users can view the available products, get to know the current selling price of the product and buy or sell them based on how many kilos they want. Once the order is placed by the user, our delivery team will dispatch it to your requested location.

  • View Available products.
  • Know current selling price
  • Buy/sell the products
  • Delivery of the product
